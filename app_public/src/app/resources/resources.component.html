Available Resources

Below are available resources that may be beneficial for you and your children during the pandemic.

Reading Eggs


Reading Eggs program focuses on a core reading curriculum of skills and strategies essential for sustained reading success. Reading Eggs offers a 30-day free trial to test out their program. After the free trial, there's a monthly $9.95 fee to use Reading Eggs. Reading Eggs are for students between the ages of 2-13 years old.

Duolingo


Duolingo is a fun language-learning resource for those who want to learn a new language. They offer fun bite-size lessons feel more like a game than a textbook. This resource is for students age 10 and up. Doulingo is a free.

LYNDA


Lynda.com is a leading online learning platform that helps anyone learn business, software, technology and creative skills to achieve personal and professional goals. LYNDA offers a free 30-day trial to test out the courses available on their site. After the free trial, there's a $29.99 monthly fee. LYNDA is more for students who are in high school and up.

Lightbot: Code Hour


Lightbot - Code Hour is meant to introduce kids who have no experience whatsoever programming, and is all-ages friendly! That means anyone anywhere from K to 12 can play, have fun and learn real programming logic!

edX


edX is the trusted platform for education and learning. edX is reimagining the possibilities of education, providing the highest-quality, stackable learning experiences including the groundbreaking MicroMasters® programs. Depending on what course you chose from, some may be free and some may not.

Photomath


Learn math, check homework and study for upcoming tests and ACTs/SATs with the most used math learning app in the world! Got tricky homework or class assignments? Get unstuck ASAP with our step-by-step explanations and animations.Photomath is free and works without wi-fi or data. Whether you’re on the bus, in a cave, or just offline, core Photomath is always available. We’ve got you covered from basic arithmetic to advanced calculus and geometry. You CAN do math!
